About Me

Software Developer

I have always wanted to become a software developer. My goal is to create software that is simple and fun to use. Growing up, I was fan of video games and was interested in learning how they worked and how they were created. This curiousity helped me learn more about software development in programming classes I took in junior high, high school, and college.

Education

I graduated BYU-I in April 2025 with a Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering, emphasis in Design. I learned a lot about how to design and code programs of various sizes using the concepts of encapsulation, inheritance, and separation of concerns. I had the opportunity to be in leadership positions for many of my courses, and even was a team lead in class-wide projects during my last two semesters. his taught me a lot about leading others, and how to direct a group of programmers towards a common goal.
